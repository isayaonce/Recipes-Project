Tia Amadita's Homemade Cake

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 16 good portions
Quantity Ingredients
2 cups Flour
1 1/4 cup Sugar
1/2 cup Salted Butter
2 tsp. Vanilla Extract
1/4tsp. Salt
1 cup Milk
2 Large Eggs lightly beaten

Step 1:Add the Dry Ingredients into a mixing bowl and whisk together.

Step 2:Mix Sugar,Eggs, and Butter together until creamy; Add vanilla.

Step 3:Pour Sugar Butter mixture into Dry Ingredients and beat for 2 minutes

Strawberries with Cream

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Servings: 6

Equipment

  • Paring Knife
  • Colander
  • Cutting Board
  • Serving Bowl
  • Cream Pitcher
  • Small sugar bowl

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredients
3 cups fresh strawberries
1 pint heavy cream
1/2cups sugar

Directions

  1. Use the paring knife to remove the stem and
    leaves from each strawberry.
  2. Put the strawberries into the colander and
    rinse them well under the cold water
  3. Cut any large strawberries into bite-size pieces.
    Put the strawberries into the serving bowl.
  4. Pour cream into the cream pitcher. Put sugar in
    the sugar bowl. That way your guests can put
    the amount of cream and sugar they prefer on
    their strawberries.

Lime Pie

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Servings: 12

Ingredients

Lime Filling
Quantity Ingredients
3/4 cup cornstarch
4 egg yolks (reserve whote for merengue topping
2 1/2 cups water
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
2 cups sugar
1 1/2teaspoons salt
4 Tablespoons salted butter

Directions

  1. Dissolve cornstarch in part of the water. Blend in egg yolks.
    Add remaining water and rest of the ingredients included.
    Strain into saucepan. Add butter and cook slowly, stirring
    constantly with a wooden spoon, until mixture boils and thick-
    ens.
  2. Pour into baked pastry shell
    Meringue:
    Follow recipe for Meringue in Coconut Pie, but
    add 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice after adding
    sugar and baking powder.
